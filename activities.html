Activities

Engage in a game of golf alongside an Impala.

Kiligolf, an exquisite course crafted by David Jones, offers breathtaking views of Mount Meru and Mount Kilimanjaro. Playing a round on Kiligolf is a memorable and demanding adventure, navigating lush fairways, meandering streams, and ponds, while gracefully evading Impala and Gazelle leisurely grazing on the greens.

Immerse in the serene woodland of Arusha National Park.

The Wild Fig Tree is ideally situated within an hour's drive of the captivating Arusha National Park. Embarking on day safaris within the park provides a unique chance to immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring beauty of mountainous wilderness, soda lakes, and abundant wildlife. The mountain forests come alive with the vibrant sounds of Colobus Monkeys and a variety of mountain birds, creating a truly magical experience..

Nduruma Polo Club.

The Nduruma Polo Club fosters a family-friendly environment, serving as the vibrant center of the local community. Excitement fills the air as players take to the international-sized polo field three times a week, providing endless entertainment for all.

Engage in the world of polo at the Nduruma Polo Club. From one-hour lessons for beginners to club chukkas for experienced players, everyone can participate and contribute to the growth of the sport in Tanzania.

Beyond polo, the club offers field hockey matches and club nights for non-equestrian enthusiasts. Additional amenities include a swimming pool, spa, and gym, ensuring a well-rounded experience for members and visitors alike.
